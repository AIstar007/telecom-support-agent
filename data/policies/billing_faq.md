# Billing FAQ

## Why did my bill increase this month?
Bills can increase due to: prorated charges from a mid-cycle plan change,
one-time device installment charges, overage on data/roaming beyond your
plan limit, or a promotional discount expiring.

## How do refunds work?
Refunds for billing errors are processed within 5-7 business days to the
original payment method after a specialist confirms the discrepancy.
