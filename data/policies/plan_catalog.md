# Plan Catalog

## Smart Saver — ₹399/month
- 40GB data per month
- Unlimited local + STD calls
- 100 SMS/day
- Best for: light users, single-line accounts

## Unlimited 5G — ₹599/month
- 100GB data per month, 5G included where available
- Unlimited local + STD + roaming calls (India)
- 100 SMS/day
- Free OTT subscription (1 platform, rotating monthly)
- Best for: most customers; our most popular plan

## Family Max — ₹999/month
- 300GB shared data across up to 4 lines
- Unlimited calls for all lines
- Free OTT bundle (2 platforms)
- Best for: households with 2+ active lines

## Switching plans
Plan changes take effect at the start of the next billing cycle unless the
customer explicitly requests an immediate switch, in which case charges are
prorated for the current cycle. Downgrading mid-cycle does not refund the
price difference for the current period. Switching plans does not affect
existing device installment agreements.

## Data overage
Exceeding your monthly data allowance is billed at ₹10/GB, capped at ₹300/month,
after which speed is throttled to 1 Mbps until the next cycle rather than
continuing to bill overage charges.
